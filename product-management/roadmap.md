# Strategic Roadmap: My Dog’s Well-Being

This roadmap outlines initiatives, timelines, and metrics to ensure my dog is happy, healthy, and engaged.

## Vision

My dog enjoys a healthy, stimulating, and stress-free environment whether I am home or away, feeling safe, loved, and mentally engaged.

## Strategic Goals & Initiatives by Quarter

| Quarter | Strategic Goal | Initiative | Metrics / Signals |
|---------|----------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Q1 | Health | Implement consistent feeding schedule and balanced diet | Weight stability, vet feedback |
| Q1 | Engagement | Introduce puzzle toys and treat dispensers | Toy usage, time spent engaged |
| Q1 | Independence | Begin short alone-time sessions with positive reinforcement | Calm behavior, duration tolerated |
| Q2 | Health | Schedule regular walks and exercise routines | Daily activity levels, energy levels |
| Q2 | Engagement | Set up dog camera with two-way communication | Interaction with camera, reduced boredom |
| Q2 | Socialization | Start weekly dog park visits or playdates | Positive interactions, calm social behavior |
| Q3 | Independence | Gradually increase alone time and implement calming cues | Duration tolerated alone, reduced anxiety signs |
| Q3 | Engagement | Introduce short training sessions and interactive play | Engagement, responsiveness, learning new commands |
| Q4 | Health | Vet check-up and wellness assessment | Vet feedback, overall health metrics |
| Q4 | Socialization | Neighborhood walks to interact with dogs and people | Social behavior, calmness on walks |
| Q4 | Engagement / Independence | Evaluate overall interventions and optimize | Observed happiness, activity levels, stress indicators |

## Monitoring and Review Cadence

- Weekly: Track engagement, behavior, and happiness indicators  
- Monthly: Assess progress toward strategic goals and adjust initiatives  
- Quarterly: Conduct roadmap review to evaluate success and re-prioritize initiatives  

## Why This Demonstrates Product Management Skills

- Strategic Vision: Clearly defined long-term outcome for my “product”  
- Goal Alignment: Each initiative maps to a measurable strategic goal  
- Prioritization: Focused on high-impact initiatives each quarter  
- Metrics-Driven: Success measured with observable signals  
- Iterative Planning: Regular reviews allow for adjustments and optimization

# User Persona: Orzo

- Name: Orzo NP
- Age: 3 years
- Breed: Pembroke Welsh Corgi
- Size: Medium
- Location: Urban Mid-Atlantic city with access to green spaces
-  Occupation: Full-time companion, boss of the house

## Demographics & Background

- Lives in an apartment with two active adults  
- Enjoys daily outdoor activity, light hikes, and interactive play  
- Sociable with humans and other dogs  
- Moderate energy bursts balanced with nap times  

## Goals & Motivations

- Stay active and healthy through play and walks  
- Socialize with people and other dogs  
- Receive mental stimulation and enrichment when alone  
- Strengthen bond with owners through interaction and attention  
- Explore outdoors safely and enjoy light adventures  
- Earn rewards through food motivation  

## Behaviors

- Approaches owners when seeking attention or play  
- Whines or nudges when playtime is desired and one owner is unavailable  
- Engages with puzzle toys, fetch, and tug-of-war  
- Adapts well to routine but appreciates variety in activities  
- Highly food motivated; uses food to guide behavior  
- Smart and strategic; can take advantage of situations to get what he wants  
- Affectionate but on his own terms  
- Avoids right paw being touched due to prior injury  
- Weary of vet visits but tolerates them for treats like peanut butter  
- Can become destructive when physical or mental needs are unmet  

## Pain Points / Frustrations

- Limited outdoor access when owners are busy  
- Lack of engagement or mental stimulation during work hours  
- Overstimulation from too many activities in a short time  
- Occasional separation anxiety when both owners are unavailable  
- Discomfort with right paw being touched  
- Stress or anxiety during vet visits  
- Frustration when energy or enrichment needs are unmet, leading to destructive behaviors  

## Needs

- Predictable daily schedule for exercise, play, and rest  
- Access to enrichment activities during owner unavailability  
- Food rewards to motivate training and positive behavior  
- Respect for boundaries (e.g., right paw sensitivity, affectionate interactions on his terms)  
- Social interaction with humans and other dogs  
- Moderate grooming and comfortable living environment  
- Clear communication and cues from owners  

## Personality Traits

- Bold, playful, and personable  
- Smart and strategic  
- Affectionate selectively  
- Food motivated and responsive to rewards  
- Curious and intelligent  
- Energetic but balanced with downtime  
- Independent-minded, with occasional stubborn streaks  

## Experience Goals

- Enjoy daily life in urban apartment with structured playtime  
- Maintain physical and mental health through active and enriching routines  
- Strengthen emotional bond with owners through responsive care  
- Experience consistent, safe, and stimulating adventures  
- Receive rewards and recognition for positive behavior  
- Have boundaries respected while being engaged and loved  

## PM Skills Highlighted

- User-Centric Thinking: Treating the dog as a “user” with nuanced needs, goals, and behaviors  
- Empathy & Observation: Understanding pain points, preferences, and motivations  
- Prioritization: Balancing activity, rest, mental stimulation, and boundaries  
- Experience Design: Designing routines, playtime, enrichment, and reward systems to maximize satisfaction  
- Data-Informed Decisions: Observing behaviors to inform schedules, play strategies, enrichment tools, and training approaches

## Notes

- Responsible: The person who executes the task  
- Accountable: The person ultimately accountable for success  
- Consulted: People whose input is sought  
- Informed: People who need to be kept updated  

This RACI highlights ownership, accountability, and collaboration, similar to how I would structure roles across a product initiative.
